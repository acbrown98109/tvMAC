## Brand Concept

**ApPotato** is a potato disguised as an apple:
- **Red Apple Shell** → Premium, polished exterior (what people see first)
- **Potato Inside** (revealed by bite) → Humble, practical reality
- **Tech Circuits Glitching Through** → Engineering truth breaking through the facade

**Brand Philosophy:**  
*"Not what it seems. Premium polish hiding down-to-earth engineering."*

**The Metaphor:**
- Everyone tries to look like Apple (sleek, premium, innovative)
- But underneath we're all potatoes (practical, humble, resourceful)
- The circuits show the tech trying to break free from both facades
- The bite reveals the truth: it's okay to be a potato in Apple's clothing

### Visual Story Elements:
- **Nervous expression** → The disguise isn't perfect
- **Sweat drop** → Trying hard to maintain appearances  
- **Zipper on the side** → Subtle costume detail
- **Circuits glitching through** → The tech can't be contained
- **Potato texture in bite** → The reveal moment

## Logo Design

### The ApPotato Character

A potato wearing an apple costume (that's coming apart):

1. **Red Apple Shell** (Apple gradient)
   - Polished, glossy exterior
   - Iconic leaf on top
   - Represents: Premium facade, design-first appearance

2. **The Bite Reveal** (Potato texture visible)
   - Shows brown potato skin underneath
   - Potato spots and texture exposed
   - Tiny circuits visible in the bite
   - Represents: The practical truth underneath

3. **Tech Elements Breaking Through**
   - Blue circuit lines glitching through the costume
   - Microchip "eyes" in potato dimples
   - Zipper detail on the side (barely visible)
   - Represents: Engineering reality can't be hidden

4. **Character Details**
   - Nervous smile (trying to maintain the disguise)
   - Sweat drop (the effort of keeping up appearances)
   - Potato eyes with hidden tech inside
   - Represents: Relatable imposter syndrome

## Usage Guidelines

### ✅ Correct Usage
- Maintain aspect ratio (no stretching)
- Use on light backgrounds (`#F8F9FA` or white)
- Preserve clear space around logo
- Use official SVG files (vector format)

### ❌ Incorrect Usage
- Don't change colors arbitrarily
- Don't distort or skew the character
- Don't add drop shadows or effects
- Don't use on busy backgrounds
- Don't separate the character from the text
- Don't recreate the logo from scratch
